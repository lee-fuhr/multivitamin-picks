How we choose our picks

We evaluate multivitamins by poring over ingredient labels, dosage amounts, and form factors, then cross-reference those with results from independent testing organizations like USP and ConsumerLab. Our recommendations come from published expert reviews, long-term user feedback, and brand reputation, not from our own lab tests. The most common issues we hear about are poor tablet dissolution and excessive fillers. We prioritize brands that pursue third-party certification and disclose sourcing. We also consider value: a high price tag doesn’t guarantee better absorption or efficacy.
